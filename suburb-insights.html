Our Methodology

What We Analyse in Every Suburb

Price Growth Trajectory

5-year and 10-year median price growth, current market momentum, and short/mid/long-term growth projections based on comparable suburb data.

Rental Yield Analysis

Gross and net rental yields, price-to-rent ratios, historical rental growth, and vacancy rate analysis to assess income performance.

Land Value Ratio

Land-to-asset value ratio, price per square metre analysis, and minimum lot sizes — key metrics the selling agent never volunteers.

Infrastructure Pipeline

Planned infrastructure: transport links, schools, hospitals, commercial precincts, and government capital expenditure — a critical leading indicator.

Demographic Trends

Population growth projections, demographic shifts (age, income, household composition), and migration patterns that drive housing demand.

Supply Constraints

Zoning limitations, development approvals, land availability, and new supply pipeline — understanding scarcity is key to understanding long-term price pressure.

Who Is RICO Buyers Agent?

Born from a frustration. Built for buyers. We exist because the property industry was designed for sellers — and we decided to change that.

Our Story

We Levelled the Playing Field for Buyers

For decades, the Australian property market has been set up to favour vendors. Selling agents — skilled, well-resourced, and highly motivated — represent the seller's interests exclusively. The buyer walks into negotiations uninformed, emotionally invested, and often overpaying.

RICO Buyers Agent was founded with one belief: buyers deserve the same level of expert representation that sellers have always enjoyed. We built our agency from the ground up to be the antidote to misinformation, overpricing, and the emotional impulse buying that costs Australian buyers millions every year.

Today, RICO operates across all of Australia with a data-driven, buyer-only model that has secured over 90 properties and saved our clients millions in purchase price reductions and avoided mistakes.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't just words on a website. They're the principles that guide every property decision we make for our clients.

Buyer-Only Focus

We never act as a selling agent. Zero conflicts of interest, ever. Our legal and ethical obligation is exclusively to you, the buyer — full stop.

Data Over Emotion

Property decisions should be driven by analysis, not feeling. We provide the facts, comparable data, and expert perspective to cut through the noise.

Education First

An informed buyer makes better decisions. We educate our clients at every stage — not to sell them something, but to empower their decision-making.

Long-Term Wealth

We don't just secure a property — we help you build wealth. Every recommendation considers long-term growth, rental yield, and portfolio strategy.

The RICO Difference

5 Reasons Buyers Choose RICO

01
We only work for buyers — full stop

Unlike many agencies that offer both selling and buying services, RICO operates exclusively as a buyers agency. There is no scenario in which we represent a vendor. This means every recommendation, every inspection, and every negotiation is 100% in your interest.

02
Off-market access most buyers will never see

Our network of 500+ selling agents across Australia means RICO clients get first access to properties before they hit the open market. Approximately 70% of what we secure for clients never appears on any public listing platform.

03
Data that drives results, not gut feeling

Every RICO recommendation is backed by 30+ data points — growth trends, rental yield history, infrastructure timelines, demographic shifts, and comparable sales. We buy on the numbers, not the aesthetics.

04
Negotiation that consistently beats the market

Our advocates are trained negotiators who know every tactic selling agents use to inflate price. On average, RICO clients pay meaningfully below the vendor's initial asking price — and below comparable sales in the same area.

05
We work globally — no country required

Whether you're in Sydney or Singapore, Melbourne or Miami — RICO's remote buyer program means you can purchase Australian property with full peace of mind without being physically present. Video inspections, digital contracts, and real-time reporting.
